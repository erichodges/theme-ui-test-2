### [<](/Chapter-4/4-4) 4-5 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-6/)

# pravṛtti-bhede prayojakaṃ cittam-ekam-anekeṣām

### प्रवृत्तिभेदे प्रयोजकं चित्तमेकमनेकेषाम्

#### Although division in finer cognition, one psyche is the initiator of many

<br />

**pravṛtti** - finer cognition; special cognition

**bheda** - divide, pierce

**prayojaka** - initiating, occasioning, causing, effecting, leading to, inciting, stimulating, deputing

**citta** - psyche (the totality of the human mind, conscious and unconscious); mind; heart

**eka** - one

**aneka** - other
