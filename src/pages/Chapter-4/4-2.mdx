# jāti-antara-pariṇāmaḥ prakṛti-āpūrāt

### जात्यन्तरपरिणामः प्रकृत्यापूरातः

#### The transformation into other existences is due to the flow from the process of matter

<br />

**jāti** - existence

**antara** - other

**pariṇāma** - transformation

**prakṛti** - the original producer of the material world; nature or process of matter; nature, character, constitution, temper, disposition

**āpūrā** - flow
