### [<](/Chapter-4/4-30/) 4-31 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-32/)

# tadā sarva-āvaraṇa-mala-apetasya jñānasya-ānantyāt-jñeyam-alpam

### तदा सर्वावरणमलापेतस्य ज्ञानस्यानन्त्यात् ज्ञेयमल्पम्

#### Then, free from all dust, little to be known, due to the endlessness of knowing

<br />

**tadā** - then

**sarva** - all

**āvaraṇa** - covering

**mala** - dust; dirt

**apeta** - removed off; gone away, departed; disappeared; free from

**jñāna** - knowing; knowledge; higher knowledge

**ānantya** - due to endlessness

**jñeya** - to be known

**alpa** - little
