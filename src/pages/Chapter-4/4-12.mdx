### [<](/Chapter-4/4-11/) 4-12 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-13/)

# atīta-anāgataṃ svarūpataḥ asti-adhva-bhedāt-dharmāṇām

### अतीतानागतं स्वरूपतोऽस्तिअध्वभेदाद् धर्माणाम्

#### In their own character, past and yet to come exists due to divisions in the way of nature

<br />

**atīta** - past

**anāgata** - not yet come

**svarūpa** - own character

**asti** - exists

**adhvan** - way

**bheda** - divide, pierce

**dharma** - essential qualities; highest duty
