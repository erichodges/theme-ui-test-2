### [<](/Chapter-4/) 4-1 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-2/)

# janma-oṣadhi-mantra-tapaḥ samādhi-jāḥ siddhayaḥ

### जन्मौषधिमन्त्रतपः समाधिजाः सिद्धय

#### Accomplishments arise from birth, blessed herbs, sacred formulae, self-discipline and integration

<br />

**janman** - birth <br />

**oṣadhi** - blessed herbs, medicinal herbs <br />

**mantra** - sacred formula <br />

**tapas** - to heat; religious austerity; self-discipline; pain, suffering <br />

**samādhi** - putting together; communion; combining; integration <br />

**ja** - born <br />

**siddhi** - accomplishment, performance, fulfilment, attainment, success

<br />

#### Commentary by TKV Desikachar:

The Yoga Sūtra in Chapter Four verse One indicate five ways to reach the highest.
The fifth is the most laboured because we must start from the bottom.
– TKV Desikachar Madras December 19th 1988
