# pariṇāma-ekatvāt-vastu-tattvam

### परिणामैकत्वात् वस्तुतत्त्वम्

#### From the uniqueness of transformation, the thatness of a substance

<br />

**pariṇāma** - transformation

**ekatvā** - uniqueness

**vastu** - any really existing or abiding substance or essence, thing, object

**tattva** - principle; thatness
