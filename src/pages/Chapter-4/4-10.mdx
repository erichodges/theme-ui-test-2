### [<](/Chapter-4/4-9) 4-10 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-11/)

# tāsām-anāditvaṃ cā-āśisaḥ nityatvāt

### तासामनादित्वं चाशिषो नित्यत्वात्

#### And the beginninglessness of these is due to the eternality of primordial desire

<br />

**tāsām** - of these

**anāditva** - beginninglessness

**ca** - and

**āśis** - premordial desire

**nityatvā** - eternality
