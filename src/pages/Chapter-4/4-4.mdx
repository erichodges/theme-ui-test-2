# nirmāṇa-cittāni-asmitā-mātrāt

### निर्माणचित्तान्यस्मितामात्रात्

#### Psyches are created only from the sense of ‘I’ am-ness

<br />

**nirmāṇa** - created

**citta** - psyche (the totality of the human mind, conscious and unconscious); mind; heart

**asmitā** - egoity; the sense of 'I' am-ness

**mātrā** - only; measure, quantity, sum, size, duration
