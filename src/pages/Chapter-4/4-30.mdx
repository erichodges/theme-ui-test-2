### [<](/Chapter-4/4-29) 4-30 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-31/)

# tataḥ kleśa-karma-nivṛttiḥ

### ततः क्लेशकर्मनिवृत्तिः

#### From that cessation of afflicted action

<br />

**tatas** - hence, from that

**kleśa** - afflictions; distress; anguish

**karman** - act, action, performance; work, labour, activity; any religious act or rite; organ of sense

**nivṛtti** - cessation
