### [<](/Chapter-4/4-12) 4-13 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-14/)

# te vyakta-sūkṣmāḥ guṇa-ātmānaḥ

### ते व्यक्तसूक्ष्माः गुणात्मानः

#### These attributes of nature have visible and subtle essences

<br />

**te** - these

**vyakta** - caused to appear, manifested, apparent, visible, evident

**sūkṣma** - subtle; atomic; intangible

**guṇa** - attributes of nature; qualities

**ātman** - essence; the highest personal principle of life; the individual soul, self
