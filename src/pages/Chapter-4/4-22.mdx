### [<](/Chapter-4/4-21) 4-22 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-23/)

# citeḥ apratisaṃkramāyāḥ tat-ākāra-āpattau sva-buddhi-saṃvedanam

### चितेरप्रतिसंक्रमायाः तदाकारापत्तौ स्वबुद्धि संवेदनम्

#### Assuming that form of non-changing awareness, experience of own comprehension

<br />

**cit** - awareness, to cause to comprehend, to observe, perceive

**apratisaṃkrama** - non-changing

**tat** - that, this

**ākāra** - form / shape

**āpatti** - assumes

**sva** - own; one's self

**buddhi** - the intellectual faculty; comprehension; intelligence, discernment, judgement

**saṃvedanā** - to experience; completely knowing
