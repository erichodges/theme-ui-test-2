### [<](/Chapter-4/4-19/) 4-20 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-21/)

# eka-samaye ca-ubhaya-anavadhāraṇam

### एक समये चोभयानवधारणम्

#### And both not ascertained at one juncture

<br />

**eka** - one

**samaya** - circumstances; appointed or proper time; agreement; regulate

**ca** - and

**ubhaya** - both

**anavadhāraṇa** - not ascertained
