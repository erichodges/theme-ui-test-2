### [<](/Chapter-4/4-27) 4-28 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-29/)

# hānaṃ eṣāṃ kleśavat-uktam

### हानमेषां क्लेशवदुक्तम्

#### It is said the giving up of these is as for the afflictions

<br />

**hāna** - the act of abandoning, relinquishing, giving up, escaping, getting rid of; gone or departed; cessation

**eṣām** - of these

**kleśa** - afflictions; distress; anguish

**ukta** - it is said
