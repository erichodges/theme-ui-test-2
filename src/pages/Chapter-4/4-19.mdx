### [<](/Chapter-4/4-18) 4-19 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-20/)

# na tat-svābhāsaṃ dṛśyatvāt

### न तत्स्वाभासं दृश्यत्वात्

#### That, not own luminosity, due to the quality of the seen

<br />

**na** - no; not; nor, neither

**tat** - that, this

**sva** - own; one's self

**bhāsa** - luminosity

**dṛśyatva** - due to the quality of the seen
