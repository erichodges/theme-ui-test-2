### [<](/Chapter-4/4-7/) 4-8 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-9/)

# tataḥ tat-vipāka-anuguṇānām-eva-abhivyaktiḥ vāsanānām

### ततः तद्विपाकानुग्णानामेवाभिव्यक्तिः वासनानाम्

#### From that, indeed the manifestation of latent impressions that have similar qualities to the result

<br />

**tatas** - hence, from that

**tat** - that, this

**vipāka** - results

**anuguṇa** - similar qualities

**eva** - thus

**abhivyakti** - manifestation

**vāsanā** - latent impressions

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

“Vāsanā is an unconscious motivation directed towards
satisfying a physiological or psychological need.”
