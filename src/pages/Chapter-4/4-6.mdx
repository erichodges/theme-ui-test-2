# tatra dhyāna-jam-anāśayam

### तत्र ध्यानजमनाशयम्

#### Of these, what is born from meditation is without seeds

<br />

**tatra** - there

**dhyāna** - meditation; mental representation of the personal attributes of a deity

**ja** - born

**anāśaya** - without seeds, deposits
