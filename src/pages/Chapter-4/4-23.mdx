### [<](/Chapter-4/4-22/) 4-23 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/4-24/)

# draṣṭṛ-dṛṣya-uparaktaṃ cittaṃ sarva-artham

### द्रष्टृदृश्योपरक्तं चित्तं सर्वार्थम्

#### The all purpose psyche is coloured by the seer and the seen

<br />

**draṣṭṛ** - seer

**dṛśya** - the seen

**uparakta** - coloured

**citta** - psyche (the totality of the human mind, conscious and unconscious); mind; heart

**sarva** - all

**artha** - purpose, aim; meaning; thing, object
