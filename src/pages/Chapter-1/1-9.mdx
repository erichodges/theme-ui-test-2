### [<](/Chapter-1/1-8) 1-9 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-1/1-10/)

# śabda-jñāna-anupātī vastu-śūnyaḥ vikalpaḥ

#### शब्दज्ञानानुपाती वस्तुशून्योविकल्पः

#### Imagination is knowledge following words empty of substance

<br />

**śabda** - words, sound, language <br />

**jñāna** - knowing; knowledge; higher knowledge <br />
**anupātin** - following <br />

**vastu** - any really existing or abiding substance or essence, thing, object <br />

**śūnya** - empty <br />

**vikalpa** - false notion, fancy, imagination <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

Imagination is knowledge that comes from words empty of substance.
