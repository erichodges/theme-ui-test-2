### 1-45

# sūkṣma-viṣayatvaṃ ca-aliṅga-paryavasānam

#### सूक्ष्मविषयत्वमं चालिङ्गपर्यवसानम्

#### And the ultimate limit of subtle objects is the unmanifest

<br />

**sūkṣma** - subtle; atomic; intangible <br />

**viṣaya** - object <br />

**ca** - and <br />

**aliṅga** - unmanifest <br />

**paryavasāna** - ultimate limit <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

The ultimate limit of gross and subtle objects,
as choices for meditational unity,
is the unmanifest state of matter.
