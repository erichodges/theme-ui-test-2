### 1-36

# viśokā vā jyotiśmatī

#### विशोका वा ज्योतिष्मती

#### Or, on that luminescence which is free from sorrow

<br />

**viśokā** - free from sorrow <br />

**vā** - or <br />

**jyotiśmant** - luminescence <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

Or it can be on that inner radiance which is free from sorrow.
