# kleśa-karma-vipāka-āśayaiḥ aparāmṛṣṭaḥ puruṣa-viśeṣa īśvaraḥ

#### क्लेशकर्मविपाकाशयैःपरामृष्टः पुरुषविशेष ईश्वरः

#### The lord is a distinctive animating principle, untouched by afflictions, actions, results and deposits

<br />

**kleśa** - afflictions; distress; anguish <br />

**karman** - act, action, performance; work, labour, activity; any religious act or rite; organ of sense <br />

**vipāka** - results <br />

**āśaya** - seed, deposit; resting place <br />

**aparāmṛṣṭa** - untouched <br />

**puruṣa** - animating principle, self, consciousness, spirit; a person, man, a human being; people <br />

**viśeṣa** - distinction, difference between; characteristic difference, peculiar mark, special property, speciality, peculiarity <br />

**īśvara** - the lord; the supreme soul; personal god; special self; ruler of choices <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by T Krishnamacharya:

Through devotion and surrender to Īśvara
and by following the eight limbs of Aṣṭāṅga Yoga,
the benefits of Samādhi are realised with as little effort
as it takes to hold a pea in the palm of your hand.

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

This highest self is distinctive in its awareness,
untouched by
afflictions,
actions,
fruits and
seeds.
