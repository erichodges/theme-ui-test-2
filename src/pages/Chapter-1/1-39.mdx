# yathā-abhimata-dhyānāt-vā

#### यथाभिमतध्यानाद्वा

#### Or, from meditation on what is appropriate

<br />

**yathā** - according as <br />

**abhimata** - appropriate <br />

**dhyāna**- meditation; mental representation of the personal attributes of a deity <br />

**vā** - or <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey: <br />

Or it can be from meditation on what is appropriate.
