### 1-37

# vīta-rāga-viṣayaṃ vā cittam

#### वीतरागविषयम् वा चित्तम्

#### Or, as an object, a psyche free from attraction

<br />

**vīta** - free from; smooth <br />

**rāga** - colour; attraction; delight in; vehement desire of; attachment; a musical note, harmony, melody <br />

**viṣaya** - object <br />

**vā** - or <br />

**citta** - psyche (the totality of the human mind, conscious and unconscious); mind; heart <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

Or it can be taking as an object, a psyche free from the distractions of attraction.
