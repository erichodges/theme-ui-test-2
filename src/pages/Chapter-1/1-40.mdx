### [<](/Chapter-1/1-39) 1-40 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-1/1-41/)

# parama-aṇu-parama-mahattva-antaḥ asya vaśīkāraḥ

#### परमाणुपरममहत्त्वान्तोऽस्य वशीकारः

#### A person has mastery from the most minute to the most greatest

<br />

**parama** - highest, most <br />

**aṇu** - minute <br />

**mahattva** - greatest <br />

**anta** - end (from ... to) <br />

**asya** - his <br />

**vaśīkāra** - mastery <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey: <br />

A person who has mastery of concentration can range their attention, whether on subtle objects, or on gross objects.
