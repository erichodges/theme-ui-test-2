### 1-25

# tatra niratiśayaṃ sarva-jña-bījam

#### तत्र निरतिशयं सर्वज्ञबीजम्

#### There the seed of all knowing is unsurpassed

<br />

**tatra** - there <br />

**niratiśaya** - unsurpassed <br />

**sarva** - all <br />

**jña** - knowing <br />

**bīja** - seed <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

Within it the seed of omniscience is unsurpassed.
