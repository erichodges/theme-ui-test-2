# ṛtaṃbharā tatra prajñā

#### ऋतंभरा तत्र प्रज्ञा

#### There the knowing bears truth

<br />

**ṛta** - truth <br />

**bharā** - carries <br />

**tatra** - there <br />

**prajñā** - knowing; insight <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

From that, the experience of knowing carries a universal truth.
