# tasya vācakaḥ praṇavaḥ

#### तस्य वाचकः प्रणवः

#### Its expression is sacred

<br />

**tasya** - its <br />

**vācaka** - expression; verbal , expressed by words; speaking, saying, telling anythin <br />

**praṇava** - sacred syllable <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

Its syllables are sacred.
