# 1-21

# tīvra-saṃvegānām-āsannaḥ

### प्रमाणविपर्ययविकल्पनिद्रास्मृतयः

#### It is near for those with intense ardour

<br />

**tīvra** - intense <br />

**saṃvega** - ardour; excitement <br />

**āsanna** - near <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by T Krishnamacharya:

There are different grades of inclination and possibility
among those who are interested to experience the state of Samādhi.

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

It is near for those with intense ardour.
