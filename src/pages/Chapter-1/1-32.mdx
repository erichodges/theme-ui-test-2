# For that purpose of counteracting them practice one principle

#### तत्प्रतिषेधार्थमेकतत्त्वाभ्यासः

#### For that purpose of counteracting them practice one principle

<br />

**tat** - that, this <br />

**pratiṣedha** - counteracting <br />

**artha** - purpose, aim; meaning; thing, object <br />

**eka** - one <br />

**tattva** - principle; thatness <br />

**abhyāsa** - practice

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

For the purpose of counteracting the distractions,
cultivate a practice on a single noble principle.
