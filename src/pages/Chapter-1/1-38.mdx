### [<](/Chapter-1/1-37/) 1-38 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-1/1-39/)

# svapna-nidrā-jñāna-ālambanaṃ vā

#### स्वप्ननिद्राज्ञानालम्बनमं वा

#### Or, by supporting with knowledge of dreams and deep sleep

<br />

**svapna** - dreams <br />

**nidrā** - deep sleep <br />

**jñāna** - knowing; knowledge; higher knowledge <br />

**ālambana** - supporting, resting on <br />

**vā** - or <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

Or it can be by resting on knowledge of dreams and deep sleep.
