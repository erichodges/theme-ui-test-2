# tasya-api nirodhe sarva-nirodhāt-nirbījaḥ samādhiḥ

#### तस्यापि निरोधे सर्वनिरोधान्निर्बीजः समाधिः

#### When even that is contained, all is contained; this is integration without seed

<br />

**tasya** - its <br />

**api** - even, also, very; something more; moreover <br />

**nirodha** - to contain, enclose; cover; confine, restrain; quell, surpress; quiesce <br />

**sarva** - all <br />

**nirbīja** - without seed <br />

**samādhi** - putting together; communion; combining; integration <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

When even these tendencies of knowing are contained all is contained;
this is integration without seed.
