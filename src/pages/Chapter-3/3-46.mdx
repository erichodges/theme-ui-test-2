### [<](/Chapter-3/3-45) 3-46 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-47/)

# grahaṇa-svarūpa-asmitā-anvaya-arthavattva-saṃyamāt-indriya-jayaḥ

#### From complete restraint on the process of grasping, own character, sense of ‘I’ am-ness, connection, significance; mastery of senses

<br />

**grahaṇa** - the process of grasping

**svarūpa** - own character

**asmitā** - egoity; the sense of 'I' am-ness

**anvaya** - connection

**arthavattva** - significance, importance

**saṃyama** - complete restraint

**indriya** - faculty of sense; sense; organ of sense

**jaya** - mastery; conquest, victory, triumph, winning
