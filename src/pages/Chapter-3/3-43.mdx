### [<](/Chapter-3/3-42) 3-43 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-44/)

# bahiḥ akalpitā vṛttiḥ mahā-videhā tataḥ prakāśa-āvaraṇa-kṣayaḥ

#### External non-imaginary fluctuation is the great discarnate, hence the covering of the light diminishes

<br />

**bahis** - external

**akalpitā** - non-imaginary

**vṛtti** - fluctuation; modification; turning; set in motion, course of action, behaviour, movement; activity, function; profession

**mahā** - great

**videha** - discarnate

**tatas** - hence, from that

**prakāśa** - brightness

**āvaraṇa** - covering

**kṣaya** - diminish, wasting or wearing away
