### [<](/Chapter-3/3-50) 3-51 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-52/)

# sthāni-upanimantraṇe saṅga-smaya-akaraṇaṃ punaḥ aniṣṭa-prasaṅgāt

#### When invitation from high position, not effecting association and conceit, again towards unwanted association

<br />

**upanimantraṇa** - invitation

**saṅga** - association

**smaya** - conceit

**akaraṇa** - not making, effecting

**punar** - again

**aniṣṭa** - unwanted, undesirable, unfavourable

**prasaṅga** - towards association
