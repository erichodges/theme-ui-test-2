### [<](/Chapter-3/3-49/) 3-50 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-51/)

# tat-vairāgyāt-api doṣa-bīja-kṣaye kaivalyam

#### That dispassion also diminish seed defect, abstraction

<br />

**tat** - that, this

**vairāgya** - dispassion

**api** - even, also, very; something more; moreover

**doṣa** - affection, defect, fault, biological humours

**bīja** - seed

**kṣaya** - diminish, wasting or wearing away

**kaivalya** - independence, freedom, solitude, separateness, abstraction, not connected with anything else; uncompounded, unmingled
