### [<](/Chapter-3/3-54) 3-55 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-4/)

# sattva-puruṣayoḥ śuddhi-sāmye kaivalyam

#### Equality of purity, in clarity and animating principle, is abstraction

<br />

**sattva** - clarity

**puruṣa** - animating principle, self, consciousness, spirit; a person, man, a human being; people

**śuddha** - pure, cleansed, cleared, clean, clear, free from

**sāmya** - equal

**kaivalya** - independence, freedom, solitude, separateness, abstraction, not connected with anything else; uncompounded, unmingled
