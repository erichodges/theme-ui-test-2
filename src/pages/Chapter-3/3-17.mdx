### [<](/Chapter-3/3-16) 3-17 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-18/)

# śabda-artha-pratyayānām-itaretara-adhyāsāt-saṃkaraḥ tat-pravibhāga-saṃyamāt-sarva-bhūta-rūta-jñānam

#### Overlapping, one with the other, words, purpose, psychic activity, completely mixed; complete restraint on separation of that, knowledge of the cry of all beings. Complete restraint on the three transformations, knowledge of past and yet to come

<br />

**śabda** - words, sound, language

**artha** - purpose, aim; meaning; thing, object

**pratyaya** - psychic activity; cause; conception, assumption, notion, idea;

**itaretara** - one with the other

**adhyāsā** - overlapping

**saṃkara** - completely mixed

**tat** - that, this

**pravibhāga** - separation

**saṃyama** - complete restraint

**sarva** - all

**bhūta** - element; living being

**rūta** - cry of

**jñāna** - knowing; knowledge; higher knowledge
