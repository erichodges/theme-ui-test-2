### [<](/Chapter-3/3-35) 3-36 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-37/)

# tataḥ prātibha-śrāvaṇa-vedana-ādarśa-āsvāda-vārtāḥ jāyante

#### From that are born intuitive hearing, touching, seeing, tasting, smelling

<br />

**tatas** - hence, from that

**prātibhā** - intuition

**śrāvaṇa** - hearing

**vedana** - touching

**ādarśa** - seeing

**āsvāda** - tasting

**vārtā** - smelling

**jāyante** - are born
