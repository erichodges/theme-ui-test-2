# pravṛtti-āloka-nyāsāt-sūkṣma-vyavahita-viprakṛṣṭa-jñānam

#### Placing the splendour of finer cognition, knowledge of subtle, concealed and distant

<br />

**pravṛtti** - finer cognition; special cognition

**āloka** - splendour

**nyāsa** - placing

**sūkṣma** - subtle; atomic; intangible

**vyavahita** - concealed

**viprakṛṣṭa** - distant

**jñāna** - knowing; knowledge; higher knowledge
