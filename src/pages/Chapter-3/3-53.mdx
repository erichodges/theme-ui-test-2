### [<](/Chapter-3/3-52/) 3-53 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-54/)

# jāti-lakṣaṇa-deśaiḥ anyatā-anavacchedāt-tulyayoḥ tataḥ pratipattiḥ

#### From that ascertainment of sameness, not separated by difference, existence, characteristics, place.

<br />

**jāti** - existence

**lakṣaṇa** - characteristic, attribute

**deśa** - place, point, region, spot

**anyatā** - difference

**anavacchedā** - not separated

**tulya** - sameness

**tatas** - hence, from that

**pratipatti** - ascertainment
