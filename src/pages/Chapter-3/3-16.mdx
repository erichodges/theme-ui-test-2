# pariṇāma-traya-saṃyamāt-atīta-anāgata-jñānam

#### Complete restraint on the three transformations, knowledge of past and yet to come

<br />

**pariṇāma** - transformation

**traya** - the three

**saṃyama** - complete restraint

**atīta** - past

**anāgata** - not yet come

**jñāna** - knowing; knowledge; higher knowledge
