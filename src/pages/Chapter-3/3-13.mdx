### [<](/Chapter-3/3-12/) 3-13 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-14/)

# etena bhūta-indriyeṣu-dharma-lakṣaṇa-avasthā-pariṇāmā-vyākhyātāḥ

#### By this transformation is essential qualities, characteristics and state in the elements and senses explained

<br />

**etena** - by this

**bhūta** - element; living being

**indriya** - faculty of sense; sense; organ of sense

**dharma** - essential qualities; highest duty

**lakṣaṇa** - characteristic, attribute

**avasthā** - state

**pariṇāma** - transformation

**vyākhyātā** - explained
