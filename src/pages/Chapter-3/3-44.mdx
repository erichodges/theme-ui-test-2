### [<](/Chapter-3/3-43) 3-44 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-45/)

# sthūla-svarūpa-sūkṣma-anvaya-arthavattva-saṃyamāt-bhūta-jayaḥ

#### From complete restraint grossness, own character, subtlety, connection, significance; mastery of elements

<br />

**sthūla** - gross, tangible, material; large, thick, stout, massive, bulky, big, huge

**svarūpa** - own character

**sūkṣma** - subtle; atomic; intangible

**anvaya** - connection

**arthavattva** - significance, importance

**saṃyama** - complete restraint

**bhūta** - element; living being

**jaya** - mastery; conquest, victory, triumph, winning
