# nābhi-cakre kāya-vyūha-jñānam

#### On navel wheel, knowledge of arrangement of body

<br />

**nābhi** - navel

**cakra** - wheel

**kāya** - the body

**vyūha** - arrangement, placing apart, distribution; orderly arrangement of the parts of a whole;

**jñāna** - knowing; knowledge; higher knowledge
