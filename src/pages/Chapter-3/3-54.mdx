### [<](/Chapter-3/3-53/) 3-54 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-55/)

# tārakaṃ sarva-viṣayaṃ sarvathā-viṣayam-akramaṃ ca-iti viveka-jaṃ jñānam

#### Thus knowing born of discrimination, liberating all objects, objects in every way and non-sequential

<br />

**tāraka** - liberating

**sarva** - all

**viṣaya** - an object; any object of affection or concern or attention

**sarvathā** - in every way, at all times

**akrama** - non-sequential

**ca** - and

**iti** - thus; in this manner; something that has been said or thought; having so said; (it is used like a quotation mark)

**viveka** - discrimination, discernment; the faculty of distinguishing and classifying things according to their real properties; the power of separating the invisible Spirit from the visible world (or spirit from matter); truth from untruth

**ja** - born

**jñāna** - knowing; knowledge; higher knowledge
