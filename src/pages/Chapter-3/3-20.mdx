### [<](/Chapter-3/3-19/) 3-20 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-21/)

# na ca tat-sa-ālambanaṃ tasya-aviṣayī-bhūtatvāt

#### And not that supporting, its being-ness not an object

<br />

**na** - no; not; nor, neither

**ca** - and

**tat** - that, this

**ālambana** - supporting, resting on

**tasya** - its

**aviṣayī** - not object

**bhūtatvā** - elementalism; being-ness
