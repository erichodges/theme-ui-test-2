### [<](/Chapter-3/3-36/) 3-37 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-38/)

# te samādhau-upasargāḥ vyutthāne siddhayaḥ

#### These impediments to integration, accomplishments to turned outwards.

<br />

**te** - these

**samādhi** - putting together; communion; combining; integration

**upasargā** - impediments

**vyutthāna** - turned outward; swerving from the right course; rising up

**siddhi** - accomplishment, performance, fulfilment, attainment, success
