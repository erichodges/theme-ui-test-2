### [<](/Chapter-3/3-41) 3-42 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-43/)

# kāya-ākāśayoḥ saṃbandha-saṃyamāt-laghu-tūla-samāpatteḥ ca-ākāśa-gamanam

#### From complete restraint of relationship of body and space and unity with lightness and cotton, travel through space

<br />

**kāya** - the body

**ākāśa** - space; ether; subtle and ethereal fluid; sky, atmosphere

**saṃbandha** - connection

**saṃyama** - complete restraint

**laghu** - lightness

**tūla** - cotton

**samāpatti** - coming together, unity

**ca** - and

**gamana** - travel
