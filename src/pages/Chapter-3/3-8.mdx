### [<](/Chapter-3/3-7/) 3-8 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-9/)

# tad-api bahir-aṅgaṃ nirbījasya

#### That also external limb to without seed

<br />

**tat** - that, this

**api** - even, also, very; something more; moreover

**bahis** - external

**aṅga** - limb

**nirbīja** - without seed
