# śrotra-ākāśayoḥ saṃbandha-saṃyamāt-divyaṃ śrotram

#### From complete restraint of connection between ear and space; divine hearing

<br />

**śrotra** - sense of hearing; ear

**ākāśa** - space; ether; subtle and ethereal fluid; sky, atmosphere

**saṃbandha** - connection

**saṃyama** - complete restraint

**divya** - divine
