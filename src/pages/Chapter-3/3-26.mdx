# bhuvana-jñānaṃ sūrye saṃyamāt

#### Complete restraint on the sun, knowledge of the world

<br />

**bhuvana** - world

**jñāna** - knowing; knowledge; higher knowledge

**sūrya** - sun

**saṃyama** - complete restraint
