### [<](/Chapter-3/3-38/) 3-39 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-40/)

# udāna-jayāt-jala-paṅka-kaṇṭaka-ādiṣu-asaṅgaḥ ukrāntiḥ ca

#### From mastery of the upward breath, a going above and non-association with water, mud, thorns and so forth

<br />

**udāna** - upward air

**jaya** - mastery; conquest, victory, triumph, winning

**jala** - water

**paṅka** - mud

**kaṇṭaka** - thorns

**ādi** - and the rest

**asaṅga** - non-association

**ukrānti** - going above

**ca** - and
