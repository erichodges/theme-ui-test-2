### [<](/Chapter-3/3-46/) 3-47 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-48/)

# tataḥ manaḥ javitvaṃ vikaraṇa-bhāvaḥ pradhāna-jayaḥ ca

#### From that fleetness of mind, becoming without instruments and mastery over unevolved matter

<br />

**tatas** - hence, from that

**manas** - mind

**javitva** - fleetness

**vikaraṇa** - without instruments

**bhāva** - becoming

**pradhāna** - prime; chief; major; the most important or essential part; unevolved or root of matter, original source; the creative principle of nature

**jaya** - mastery; conquest, victory, triumph, winning

**ca** - and
