### [<](/Chapter-3/3-44) 3-45 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-3/3-46/)

# tataḥ aṇima-ādi-prādurbhāvaḥ kāya-saṃpat-tat-dharma-anabhigātaḥ ca

#### From that appearance of minuteness and so forth, bodily perfection and not troubled by its essential qualities

<br />

**tatas** - hence, from that

**aṇiman** - minuteness

**ādi** - and the rest

**prādurbhāva** - appearance

**kāya** - the body

**saṃpat** - perfection, destiny

**tat** - that, this

**dharma** - essential qualities; highest duty

**anabhighāta** - not troubled

**ca** - and
