### [<](/Chapter-2/2-52/) 2-53 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-54/)

# dhāraṇāsu ca yogyatā manasaḥ

#### धारणासु च योग्यता मनसः

#### And fitness of the mind for concentration

<br />

**dhāraṇā** - concentration

**ca** - and

**yogyatā** - fitness, ability, capability, suitableness, propriety

**manas** - mind

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

“As Prāṇāyāma dissolves the covering of the light,
fitness of the mind for concentration arises.”

“Prāṇāyāma is a key to the door of Dhāraṇā.”
