### [<](/Chapter-2/2-40/) 2-41 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-42/)

# sattva-śuddhi-saumanasya-eka-agrya-indriya-jaya-ātma-darśana-yogyatvāni ca

#### सत्त्वशुद्धिः सौमनस्यैकाग्र्येन्द्रियजयात्मदर्शन योग्यत्वानि च

#### From purity of clarity, open-mindedness, one-pointedness, mastery of the senses and capability for seeing the essence

<br />

**sattva** - clarity

**śuddha** - pure

**saumanasya** - open-mindedness

**eka** - one

**agrya** - pointedness

**indriya** - faculty of sense; sense; organ of sense

**jaya** - mastery; conquest, victory, triumph, winning

**ātman** - essence; the highest personal principle of life; the individual soul, self

**darśana** - view; seeing

**yogyatva** - capability

**ca** - and
