# tataḥ dvandva-anabhighātaḥ

#### ततो द्वङ्द्वानभिघातः

#### From that, not troubled by the pair of opposites

<br />

**tatas** - hence, from that

**dvaṃdva** - pair of opposites

**anabhighāta** - not troubled
