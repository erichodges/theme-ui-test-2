### [<](/Chapter-2/2-36/) 2-37 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-38/)

# asteya-pratiṣṭhāyāṃ sarva-ratna-upasthānam

#### अस्तेयप्रतिष्ठायां सर्वरत्नोपस्थानम्

#### When established in non-stealing, all jewels appear

<br />

**asteya** - not practicing stealing; non-stealing; less stealing

**pratiṣṭha** - established

**sarva** - all

**ratna** - jewels

**upasthāna** - appear

<br />

#### Commentary by T Krishnamacharya:

Asteya – non-stealing.
It’s not taking away that
which belongs to somebody else.
Whether Dravya, Dharma, Karma, Vidyā.
