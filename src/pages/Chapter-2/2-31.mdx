### [<](/Chapter-2/2-30/) 2-31 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-32/)

# jāti-deśa-kāla-samaya-anavacchinnāḥ sārva-bhaumāḥ mahā-vratam

#### जातिदेशकालसमयानवच्छिन्नाः सार्वभौमामहाव्रतम्

#### All stages not bound by existance, place, time or circumstance, a great vow

<br />

**jāti** - existence

**deśa** - place, point, region, spot

**kāla** - time, a period of time; season, the proper time or season for

**samaya** - circumstances; appointed or proper time; agreement; regulate

**anavacchinnā** - not bound

**sarva** - all

**bhūmi** - stage; ground; floor of a house; foundation; a place, situation

**mahā** - great

**vrata** - vow
