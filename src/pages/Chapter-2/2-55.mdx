### [<](/Chapter-2/2-54/) 2-55 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-56/)

# tataḥ paramā vaśyatā-indriyāṇām

#### ततः परमावश्यता इन्द्रियाणाम्

#### From that, best control of the senses

<br />

**tatas** - hence, from that

**parama** - highest, most

**vaśyatā** - control

**indriya** - faculty of sense; sense; organ of sense
