### [<](/Chapter-2/2-16/) 2-17 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-18/)

# draṣṭṛ-dṛśyayoḥ saṃyogaḥ heya-hetuḥ

#### द्रष्टृदृश्ययोः संयोगो हेयहेतुः

#### The cause of what must be overcome is the conjunction of the seer and the seen

<br />

**draṣṭṛ** - seer <br />

**dṛśya** - the seen <br />

**saṃyoga** - conjunction <br />

**heya** - overcome <br />

**hetu** - cause <br />

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

“We experience the world via the conjunction
of the ‘eye’ of the Cit with the ‘I’ of the Citta.”
