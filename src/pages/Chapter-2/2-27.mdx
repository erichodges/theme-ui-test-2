### [<](/Chapter-2/2-26/) 2-27 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-28/)

# tasya saptadhā-prānta-bhūmiḥ prajñā

#### तस्य सप्तधा प्रान्तभूमिः प्रज्ञ

#### Its final stage a sevenfold knowing

<br />

**tasya** - its

**saptadhā** - sevenfold

**prānta** - final

**bhūmi** - stage; ground; floor of a house; foundation; a place, situation

**prajñā** - knowing; insight
