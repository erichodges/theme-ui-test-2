### [<](/Chapter-2/2-21/) 2-22 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-23/)

# kṛta-arthaṃ prati-naṣṭam-api-anaṣṭaṃ tat-anya-sādhāraṇatvāt

#### कृतार्थं प्रतिनष्टंप्यनष्टं तदन्य साधारणत्वात्

#### With regard to its purpose done, it again disappears, even though it is not disappeared, that being common for others

<br />

**kṛta** - done; made; accomplished; performed

**artha** - purpose, aim; meaning; thing, object

**prati** - with regard to

**naṣṭa** - disappeared

**api** - even, also, very; something more; moreover

**anaṣṭa** - not disappeared

**tat** - that, this

**anya** - different from, other than

**sādhāraṇatvā** - common
