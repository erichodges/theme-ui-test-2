# ahiṃsā-satya-asteya-brahmacarya-aparigrahāḥ yamāḥ

#### अहिंसासत्यास्तेय ब्रह्मचर्यापरिग्रहाः यमाः

#### Non-harming, truthfulness, non-stealing, conservation and non-grasping are the restraints

<br />

**ahiṃsā** - non-harming; less harming

**satya** - truthful; honesty; sincere; genuine; actual

**asteya** - not practicing stealing; non-stealing; less stealing

**brahmacarya** - continence; conservation; a life stage

**aparigraha** - non-grasping; less grasping; not grasping all around; non-possessiveness; non-covetousness

**yama** - restraints

<br />

#### Commentary by TKV Desikachar:

Yama – How we relate, how we face the society we are part of.
How we link ourselves to others in speech, action, confidence.
