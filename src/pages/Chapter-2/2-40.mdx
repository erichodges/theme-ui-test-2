### [<](/Chapter-2/2-39/) 2-40 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-41/)

# śaucāt-sva-aṅga-jugupsā paraiḥ asaṃsargaḥ

#### शौचात् स्वाङ्गजुगुप्सा परैरसंसर्गः

#### From cleanliness, disdain for own body, non-association with others

<br />

**śauca** - cleanliness

**sva** - own; one's self

**aṅga** - limb

**jugupsā** - disdain

**para** - higher, other

**asaṃsarga** - non-association
