# saṃtoṣāt-anuttamaḥ sukha-lābhaḥ

#### संतोषातनुत्तमस्सुखलाभः

#### From contentment, unsurpassed happiness is attained

<br />

**saṃtoṣa** - satisfaction; contentedness with

**anuttama** - unsurpassed, incomparably the best or chief, excellent

**sukha** - pleasure; happiness; agreeable; ease; comfortable; pleasant

**lābha** - attained
