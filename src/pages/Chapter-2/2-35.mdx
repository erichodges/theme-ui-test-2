### [<](/Chapter-2/2-34/) 2-35 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-36/)

# ahiṃsā-pratiṣṭhāyāṃ tat-saṃnidhau vaira-tyāgaḥ

#### अहिंसाप्रतिष्ठायं तत्सन्निधौ वैरत्याघः

#### In the presence of that established in non-harming, animosity is forsaken

<br />

**ahiṃsā** - non-harming; less harming

**pratiṣṭha** - established

**tat** - that, this

**saṃnidhi** - in the presence

**vaira** - animosity

**tyāga** - forsaken
