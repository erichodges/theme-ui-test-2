### [<](/Chapter-2/2-23/) 2-24 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-25/)

# tasya hetuḥ avidyā

#### तस्य हेतुरविद्या

#### Its cause is illusion

<br />

**tasya** - its

**hetu** - cause

**avidyā** - illusion; spiritual ignorance; connate misidentification

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

“Better to be clear about being confused,
rather than being confused about being clear.”
