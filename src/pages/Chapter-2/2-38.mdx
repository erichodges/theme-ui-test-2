# brahmacarya-pratiṣṭhāyāṃ vīrya-lābhaḥ

#### ब्रह्मचर्य प्रतिष्ठायां वीर्यलाभः

#### When established in conservation, vigour is attained

<br />

**brahmacarya** - continence; conservation; a life stage

**pratiṣṭha** - established

**vīrya** - vigour

**lābha** - attained
