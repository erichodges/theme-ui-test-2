### [<](/Chapter-2/2-35/) 2-36 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-37/)

# satya-pratiṣṭhāyāṃ kriyā-phala-āśrayatvam

#### सत्यप्रतिष्थायं क्रियाफलाश्रयत्वम्

#### When established in truthfulness, the fruit corresponds to the activity

<br />

**satya** - truthful; honesty; sincere; genuine; actual

**pratiṣṭha** - established

**kriyā** - activity; bodily activity; medical treatment or practice

**phala** - fruit; consequence, effect; result; retribution (good or bad), gain or loss, reward or punishment, advantage or disadvantage

**āśrayatva** - corresponds
