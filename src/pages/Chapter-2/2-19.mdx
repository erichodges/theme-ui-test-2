# viśeṣa-aviśeṣa-liṅga-mātra-aliṅgāni-guṇa-parvāṇi

#### विशेषाविशेषलिङ्गमात्रालिङ्गानि गुणपर्वाणि

#### The distinct, the indistinct, the manifest alone and the unmanifest are the phases of the attributes of nature

<br />

**viśeṣa** - distinction, difference between; characteristic difference, peculiar mark, special property, speciality, peculiarity <br />

**aviśeṣa** - indistinct, without any difference, uniform <br />

**liṅga** - manifest, sign, gender <br />

**mātrā** - only; measure, quantity, sum, size, duration <br />

**aliṅga** - unmanifest <br />

**guṇa** - attributes of nature; qualities <br />

**parvan** - phase <br />
