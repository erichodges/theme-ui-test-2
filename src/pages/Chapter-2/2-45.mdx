# samādhi-siddhiḥ īśvara-praṇidhānāt

#### समाधि सिद्धिःीश्वरप्रणिधानात्

#### From dedication to the lord, accomplishment in intregration

<br />

**samādhi** - putting together; communion; combining; integration

**siddhi** - accomplishment, performance, fulfilment, attainment, success

**īśvara** - the lord; the supreme soul; personal god; special self; ruler of choices

**praṇidhāna** - dedication; respectful conduct; attention; to place down; in front of
