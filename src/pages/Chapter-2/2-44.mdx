# svādhyāyāt-iṣṭa-devatā-saṃprayogaḥ

#### स्वाध्यायादिष्टदेवता संप्रयोगः

#### From self-study, total joining together with chosen deity

<br />

**svādhyāya** - to study; to read; sacred recitation; study of the self

**iṣṭa** - chosen

**devatā** - divinity

**saṃprayoga** - total joining together
