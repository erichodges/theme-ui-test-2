### [<](/Chapter-2/2-38/) 2-39 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-40/)

# aparigraha-sthairye janma-kathaṃtā-saṃbodhaḥ

#### अपरिग्रहस्थैर्ये जन्मकथंता संबोधः

#### When steadfastness in non-grasping, understanding of the wherefore of birth

<br />

**aparigraha** - non-grasping; less grasping; not grasping all around; non-possessiveness; non-covetousness

**sthairya** - steadfastness, steadiness

**janman** - birth

**kathaṃtā** - wherefore

**saṃbodha** - understanding
