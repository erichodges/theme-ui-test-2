### [<](/Chapter-2/2-51/) 2-52 &nbsp[>](/Chapter-2/2-53/)

# tataḥ kṣīyate prakāśa-āvaraṇam

#### ततः क्षीयते प्रकाशावरणम्

#### From that, the covering of the light dissolves

<br />

**tatas** - hence, from that

**kṣīyate** - dissolves

**prakāśa** - brightness

**āvaraṇa** - covering

<br />

#### Commentary by Paul Harvey:

“From Prāṇāyāma the covering of the light dissolves.”
