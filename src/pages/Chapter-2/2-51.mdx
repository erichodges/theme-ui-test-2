# bāhya-ābhyantara-viṣaya-ākṣepī caturthaḥ

#### बाह्याभ्यन्तर विषयाक्षेपी चतुर्थः

#### The fourth transcends the external and internal as an object

<br />

**bāhya** - external

**ābhyantara** - internal

**viṣaya** - an object; any object of affection or concern or attention

**ākṣepīn** - transcends

**caturtha** - fourth
